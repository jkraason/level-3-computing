Lennard-Jones Potential:
Models Van-der-Waals interactions
U_LJ = 4*e*((s/r)**(12)-(s/r)**6)
This is true for all r (so there is a repulsive AND attractive nature to this potential)
Particles attract at moderate distances

WCA Potential:
Repulsive part of LJ potential only - equal to zero at r>=2^(1/6)s 

Key notes about parameters:
e = depth of potential well (energy scale)
sigma = particle diameter
